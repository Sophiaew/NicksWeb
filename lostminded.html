Backgrounds

Every hero has a beginning. The person they were before they started their quest. Your background helps you tell this story and helps you define the humble start to your quest. Who were you adventurer? Before you picked up the sword. Before you casted your first spell. Who were you?

Lost Minded

You are lost in this world as you have no memories of your past. You have been left with nothing besides the skills that are now muscle memory to you. Besides your name and some fleeting thoughts, everything you once knew is gone and you have no idea why.

Skill Proficiencies: Investigation, and one Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma skill of your choice
Tool Proficiencies: One artisan's tool or musical instrument of your choice
Equipment: A set of common clothes, a trinket from your past, and 5 gp

Feature: Memory Flood

Though you are unsure why, in certain interactions you get glimpses of what may have been your past. Work with your DM to determine certain events or triggers that would cause your memories to begin flooding back to you.

Reflexive Instinct

Although you are without your memories it does not mean that you have lost everything about yourself. Some behaviors and mannerisms have stuck with you, even though you can't remember why. Choose your instinctual behavior or roll on the table below.

d6 Reflexive Instinct
1 It feels natural for you to look to higher powers for aid.
2 Speaking formally feels proper.
3 You are restless and like to stay active and on the move.
4 You feel most comfortable in a quiet space.
5 A messy environment drives you to unease.
6 Being a good listener comes naturally to you.

Suggested Characteristics

A lost minded has a natural want to try and unlock the mystery that is their past. This may be their entire driving force or something they look to piece together over time. For some having no memories can make them paranoid, while others may view it as a fresh start.

Lost Minded Personality Trait

d6
1 I choose to live every moment to the fullest to enjoy the new memories I make.
2 I'm secretly scared of uncovering my past, for fear I may not like what I find.
3 I never want to forget anything ever again.
4 I am always looking for the next clue as to who I was.
5 I see this as my chance to start over.
6 It frustrates me to no end that I can't remember my past.

Lost Minded Ideal

d6
1 Fresh Start. This is the beginning of the rest of my life. (Any)
2 Helpful. I can't remember who I am, but that won't stop me from helping others. (Good)
3 Freedom. I can't be held responsible for a past I can't remember. (Chaotic)
4 Resourceful. I will use what skills I still have to contribute to society. (Lawful)
5 Selfish. I am my priority right now. I need to figure out my past. (Neutral)
6 Revenge. If I find out someone is behind my memory loss, I will viciously hunt them down. (Evil)

Ardent One Bond

d6
1 If people are willing to listen to me, I will lead them with heart and soul.
2 Someone suffered because I did not speak up. I will not make that mistake again.
3 My deity has bestowed a message upon me, it is now my sworn duty to share it with the world.
4 I respect everyone who is willing to listen to me, but there are only a few people I will trust with my secrets.
5 I can talk with just about anyone, I want to find someone special that makes me want to listen.
6 I keep my mentor's teachings in mind always and I try to spread them as often as I can.

Ardent One Flaws

d6
1 I know I'm the most charming person in the room and I dare anyone to say otherwise.
2 I talk really loudly, even when I should be quiet.
3 I don't always realize when to stop talking.
4 I always speak my mind. Always.
5 I get bored of listening to others.
6 I am terrible at keeping secrets, I love to gossip.

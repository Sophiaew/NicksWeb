Subclasses

Depending on what class you play, you will have to make a defining choice for your character at either 1st, 2nd or 3rd level. This choice is the selection of your subclass, which will provide you with an expanded arsenal of abilities not available to your base class. Whether it be your barbarian's primal path or your cleric's divine domain, this choice will be an integral moment in your character's development. This chapter will present a selection of new subclass options listed in the table below.

Subclasses

Class Subclass Level Available Description
Artificer Arcane Marksman 3rd Wield an arcane firearm with powerful forms and features
2 You accidentally stumbled into the Feywild, but could not find your way back out. When you finally escaped, you could still feel the magic of the fey inside you.
3 You fell in love with a dryad. The time you spent together infused you with fey magic.
4 A coven of hags placed a curse upon your village. Now every person in the village becomes a fey when they reach adulthood.
5 Though not fey themselves, your parents made their home in the Feywild.
6 You stole from a group of satyrs. When they finally caught you, they cursed you to become like them until they were repaid one hundred fold.

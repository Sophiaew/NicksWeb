Feats

A feat is a representation of a powerful skill or ability that a character can utilize. Feats go beyond the normal abilities received from class features. At certain levels your class gives you the Ability Score Improvement feature. Using the optional rule, you can forgo taking the feature to take a feat of your choice instead.

Spells & Magic

Much like the variety of character options, magic has numerous forms that creatures can wield and utilize. This chapter will detail a selection of new spells and magic items your characters can use and come across in their travels.

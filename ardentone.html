Backgrounds

Every hero has a beginning. The person they were before they started their quest. Your background helps you tell this story and helps you define the humble start to your quest. Who were you adventurer? Before you picked up the sword. Before you casted your first spell. Who were you?

Ardent One

You have a natural energy that springs forth from you that pulls others to your side. Your every word captivates the people you have regular conversation with. You have a natural sense of leadership and charismatic charm about you.
People feel comfortable around you and might be more willing to open up to you. Your words carry meaning.

Skill Proficiencies: Insight, Persuasion
Languages: Two of your choice
Equipment: A set of common clothes, a set of fine clothes, a diplomat's pack, and a leather pouch with 15 gp

Feature: Way with Words

The way you talk really connects with people. Whether it be large crowds or individuals, when you start talking, people are more willing to listen to you and come to your aid. If you provide them a convincing enough reason, some may even take up arms to fight alongside you.

Charming Quality

As long as you can remember there's been something about you that just draws people in. In some cases your charm even precedes you. You can roll on the following table to determine what makes you so charming or choose an option that best fits your character.

d6 Charming Quality
1 Your eyes are captivating.
2 Your voice is soothing to the ear.
3 Your smile is comforting.
4 Your presence is demanding of respect.
5 Your beauty is alluring.
6 Your energy is contagious.

Suggested Characteristics

A lost minded has a natural want to try and unlock the mystery that is their past. This may be their entire driving force or something they look to piece together over time. For some having no memories can make them paranoid, while others may view it as a fresh start.

Lost Minded Personality Trait

d6
1 I choose to live every moment to the fullest to enjoy the new memories I make.
2 I'm secretly scared of uncovering my past, for fear I may not like what I find.
3 I never want to forget anything ever again.
4 I am always looking for the next clue as to who I was.
5 I see this as my chance to start over.
6 It frustrates me to no end that I can't remember my past.

Ardent One Ideal

d6
1 Authority. Since people are so eager to listen to me, I must act as a voice of authority. (Lawful)
2 Hope. Let my voice be a proclamation of hope to those who hear it. (Good)
3 Power. If they will just blindly listen, I will lead them how I see fit. (Evil)
4 Messenger. I will spread my message however I see fit. (Neutral)
5 Improvise. An improvised speech can be better than a written one. Making decisions in the moment can be better than planning. (Chaotic)
6 Listen. Everyone has a story, sometimes its more important to listen than to speak. (Any)

Ardent One Bond

d6
1 If people are willing to listen to me, I will lead them with heart and soul.
2 Someone suffered because I did not speak up. I will not make that mistake again.
3 My deity has bestowed a message upon me, it is now my sworn duty to share it with the world.
4 I respect everyone who is willing to listen to me, but there are only a few people I will trust with my secrets.
5 I can talk with just about anyone, I want to find someone special that makes me want to listen.
6 I keep my mentor's teachings in mind always and I try to spread them as often as I can.

Ardent One Flaws

d6
1 I know I'm the most charming person in the room and I dare anyone to say otherwise.
2 I talk really loudly, even when I should be quiet.
3 I don't always realize when to stop talking.
4 I always speak my mind. Always.
5 I get bored of listening to others.
6 I am terrible at keeping secrets, I love to gossip.
